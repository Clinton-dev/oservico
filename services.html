Legal Transcription

We offer transcription of legal proceedings from spoken word and videos into a document format or printed format. We have qualified and licensed legal transcribers who are always at your service as our client to ensure quality work. The industry of legal transcription is rapidly growing and we are also expanding our services in this field. Our legal transcription services include but are not limited to transcription of depositions, testimonies, interrogations, official court hearings, client letters, legal briefings as well as general correspondence. Our legal transcribers go through a detailed process to ensure only professional output is offered. Legal transcription is very delicate and thus we ensure multiple proof-reading to ensure accuracy and grammatical integrity of the legal transcription. The transcription is also archived within our database securely and indefinitely for the client's benefit subject to acting laws and regulations and clients agreement.
